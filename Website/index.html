Can I Eat That?


Hey There!

Can I Eat That? is a Twitter bot and an Amazon Echo application that allows you to quickly and easily figure out if you are allowed, in fact, to consume that item. This app is powered by Lambda, Python, Javascript, and JSON.

To use the bot, log into Twitter and tweet @dietaryasst and list your dietary restrictions (e.g. "@dietaryasst gluten-free"). This will enable the assistant to cater to you in the future. Afterwards, to use the assistant, tweet "@dietaryasst [food] [location]" to receive notifications based on your diet.

To use the Amazon Echo application, simply say Alexa's name, list your dietary restrictions, and ask your question. e.g. “Alexa, I am lactose intolerant. Can I order a cheese pizza at Domino’s?”
